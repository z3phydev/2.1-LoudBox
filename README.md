# 2.1 LoudBox
## What is it?
A 25cm square box with 2.1 speaker system  which combins two forward-facing full-range 3.5 inch drivers with a rear-firing 8 inch subwoofer. Its sealed with internally divided enclosures. Buit in amp with accessable io for audio and power.
## Why did I make it?
I found out one of my friends dad is an audio tech, later I took apart a speaker I have to see the entrnals and with that friends dad figured out what does what.
